Egg Policy

Price

Eggs are $250 USD per dozen with USPS Priority Mail Express shipping already included within the contiguous US.

No Extras

We do not provide extras.

Guarantees

Eggs will be fresh, fertile, and well packed when shipped.

No Guarantees

There is no guarantee that eggs will arrive unbroken and there is no guaranteed hatch rate.

There will be no replacements sent for any reason.

International Shipping

In addition to the above policy, we cannot guarantee that eggs will successfully pass through customs. If they are confiscated by customs then no replacements will be sent.
